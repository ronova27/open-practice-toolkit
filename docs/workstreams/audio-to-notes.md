# Audio-To-Notes Workstream

Branch: `workstream/audio-to-notes`

## Purpose

Plan a monophonic-first audio transcription path that turns local recordings into approximate notes and rhythms with a manual correction workflow.

## Scope

- Research pitch detection, onset detection, and rhythm approximation.
- Plan approximate MIDI export.
- Define manual correction as a core part of the experience.
- Document limits clearly so the project does not overpromise AI transcription.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim perfect transcription.
- Do not upload audio or add required cloud processing.
- Do not add paid infrastructure, secrets, deployments, or analytics.
