# Video-To-Rhythm Workstream

Branch: `workstream/video-to-rhythm`

## Purpose

Plan an experimental local-first workflow for detecting visible hit or motion timing in practice videos.

## Scope

- Compare MediaPipe-style and OpenCV-style approaches.
- Plan local motion or hit timing detection.
- Explore audio alignment where practical.
- Document privacy constraints for imported videos.

## Guardrails

- Keep this module explicitly experimental.
- Do not upload videos or add required cloud processing.
- Do not add paid infrastructure, secrets, deployments, or analytics.
