What is deliberately not here yet?

This is the first vertical slice, not a finished music workstation. Notation input, practice recording, transcription, and video-to-rhythm remain separate workstreams.

The timing model is deterministic and testable, but real device timing still depends on the browser and audio hardware. Use it as a practice aid, not as a claim of laboratory precision.

Continuous transport schedules a small rolling window while this tab is active. Calibration stores only a response offset in local browser storage; no recording or upload is involved.
